OUR STORY

WE WANT TO HELP YOU TRAVEL INDEPENDENTLY AND EXPLORE AWESOME NEW DESTINATIONS.

We want to connect you with other travellers with inspiring stories and informative travel tips to make your trip even better. Not travelling yet? We want to help you plan your next holiday or that big adventure!

WE LOVE TO TRAVEL

and developed the wordoftravel concept while on a 15 month trip backpacking around Asia and Latin America. While travelling, we read a lot of travel blogs (and wrote one ourselves for friends and family) and found that most people we met were doing the same. We knew that there was a huge amount of valuable information out there which people take the time to write and that could really help other travellers - but apart from the big four or five bloggers in a region most went unread. We want to change that!

WE INCLUDE POSTS

from real independent travellers that we love, that our users have told us about and that bloggers have submitted. Our AI system monitors for new content and uses natural language processing to help classify it (smart hey!) but sometimes we ask for your help classifying it also (the human touch!).

for far more ways to help you explore the less explored parts of this awesome planet - watch this space!
